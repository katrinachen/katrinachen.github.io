Morale Compass: An Old Couple Story
斗志指南：一对老夫妇的故事

Article Length: 500 words.
Reading time: 3~4 minutes.

Let me share 3 (real) stories [我来分享三个（真实的）]

Incident 1: Today morning it was quite humid weather in Wuxi. On my way to office I saw an old couple running towards bus stop to catch a bus. The same time a bus (118 – Wuxi, Jiangsu) arrived at bus stop, passengers start getting on and off from bus. Couple ran very hard and missed the bus by 2 seconds margin. Possibly driver did not bother to look in back view mirror, passengers near the window and bystanders waiting for another bus noticed the incident and ignored.



故事1: 今早无锡的天气非常湿热。上班的路上，我看见一对老夫妇正跑向公交站赶公交。就在这时，一辆公交（江苏无锡118路）刚好抵达站台，乘客们开始上下车。老夫妇努力地追赶这辆公交，但最终还是以2秒之差错过了。可能公交司机不愿费心去看后视镜， 靠窗的乘客和旁边等其他公交的乘客们都注意到了这个现象，但却都置之不理。

Incident 2: My colleague came to pick me up. Suddenly we get caught in traffic jam at an intersection. Almost 200 cars, No cop, no traffic light. Everybody wish to go first, but all vehicles were standstill. No movement at all. I dare to get off from car and found that two other vehicle stuck and caused this heavy jam. I persuade one of the driver to go back a little to let another car pass. Finally within few minutes there was an order and everybody was moving ahead.

故事2： 我的同事开车过来接我，突然在一个交叉路口遇到了交通堵塞。几乎200辆车被堵，没有交警，没有红绿灯。每个人都想先走，但所有的车都困在原地，一点儿都没动。我大胆的从车里下来，发现有两辆车互不相让，这才造成了这次大拥堵。我劝其中一个司机退后一点，让另一辆车先过。 终于几分钟内恢复了秩序，大家都可以顺利前进了。

Incident 3: One day in January, I reached bus stop in late evening hours. It was dark, however street lights were on. There were not too many vehicle on the road. I heard a strange noise at the same time a speeding car hit the brake and then move on. It repeated several times, whenever a car passed, a sudden noise, abrupt brake and cars move on. Being curious, I get closer and noticed there was a scrap iron wire causing all this havoc. I imagined the possible consequences. Because this wire wasn’t clearly visible to drivers. This could’ve caused an accident. Accident may leads someone loss limb or life. Consequently one or more family will suffer forever. I picked up the scrap wire from the road and put it beside the road. This small initiative prevented possibly an accident, a life and much more. There were people on bus stop merely watching my actions.

故事3：元月的一天，傍晚时分我到了公交站。天很黑，还好路灯亮了。路上没有太多的车辆。突然听到了一个奇怪的声响，与此同时一辆急速前行的车突然踩了刹车，紧接着继续前行。如此，刹车、启动前行，反反复复了好多次。每每车辆经过，听到突发的噪音和突然的刹车都依旧继续前行。出于好奇，我靠近了些，注意到原来是一个废铁丝引发的这场浩劫。我设想了可能会导致的后果。因为碎铁丝对驾驶员来说并不容易看见，这样便会引发一场交通事故，而事故可能会导致人们失去肢体甚至生命，于是一个家庭甚至更多的家庭将会遭受永远的损失。我从马路上捡起废铁丝把它放在路边，这个小小的主动行为可能阻止了一场事故的发生、一个生命的丧生、甚至更多损失的降临。而公交站台等公交的人们却只是默默的看着我的举动。

Observation: Most of us are silent observer. We let things happen rather than make it happen. We’re advancing in technology. Our actions are robotic, losing morale values and empathy. When same happen to us, we simply blame system and society. We need to observe what kind of values we are passing to future generations? It is whose responsibility? We need to brainstorm, why we fail to take prompt action to favor our fellow human being?

观察：我们大多数人都是沉默的观察者。我们总是被动的等待事情自己发生，而不是主动让它发生。我们正在技术上前进，我们就像机器人一样行动，失去了士气价值和同情。 当同样的事情发生在我们身上的时候，我们只是简单地责怪体系和社会。我们需要观察我们正传递给下一代的是什么样的价值观？这是谁的责任？我们需要集思广益，为什么我们没能及时采取行动来支持我们的同伴人类呢？

Call for Action: A help B, B help C and so on. Be the first. This is the best gift we can give to our children, to future generations and create a happy and harmonious society. Start now - plant a tree of humility, care and kindness. Nurture the tree, we shall reap the fruit after our retirement.

呼吁行动：我帮你，你帮他，大家像这样彼此互相帮助。带头去做，这是我们留给子女、留给下一代最好的礼物。带头去做，我们可以创建一个幸福和谐的社会。从现在开始，种下一棵树，一颗载有谦逊、关爱与善良的树，浇灌哺育，我们终会收获幸福的果实。
